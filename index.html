Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Yahan aapke sabse common sawaalon ke jawab milenge. Aur kuch poochna ho to WhatsApp karein!

Q
Gazette Notification mein kya kya change ho sakta hai?
Naam, pita ka naam, janm tithi (DOB), pata, surname — sabhi official documents ke liye gazette legally valid hai. Aadhaar, PAN, passport, bank, property deed sab mein use hota hai.
Q
Gazette publication mein kitna samay lagta hai?
Affidavit aur documents milne ke baad, Rajasthan Gazette mein publication 15–30 working days mein hoti hai. Poori process mein humari team aapko WhatsApp updates degi.
Q
Project Report kaun kaun se banks accept karte hain?
SBI, PNB, Bank of Baroda, Canara Bank, Union Bank — sabhi nationalized banks aur NBFCs humari DPR accept karte hain. PMEGP, Mudra, CGTMSE, Standup India sab ke liye valid hai.
Q
Project Report kitne din mein milti hai?
Aapki business details milne ke 24–48 ghante ke andar PDF format mein WhatsApp par deliver kar dete hain. Emergency mein same-day delivery bhi possible hai.
Q
Payment kab karni hoti hai?
Pehle free consultation hoti hai — tab hum poori process explain karte hain. Payment sirf tab hoti hai jab aap 100% satisfied hon aur proceed karna chahein. Koi upfront charge nahi.
Q
Documents physically lene hain ya digital milenge?
Dono option available hain — WhatsApp/Email par PDF milega, aur agar chahein to physical copy Speed Post se ya Piplantri center se collect kar sakte hain.
