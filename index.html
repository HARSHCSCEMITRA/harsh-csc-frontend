    Common Questions

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Here you'll find answers to your most common questions. If you have any other questions, please WhatsApp us!

    Q
    What changes can happen in the Gazette notification?
    Name, father's name, date of birth (DOB), address, surname — the gazette is legally valid for all official documents. It is used in Aadhaar, PAN, passport, bank, property deed etc.
    Q
    How much time does it take for Gazette publication?
    After receiving the affidavit and documents, publication in the Rajasthan Gazette takes 15–30 working days. Our team will provide you with WhatsApp updates throughout the process.
    Q
    Which banks accept project reports?
    SBI, PNB, Bank of Baroda, Canara Bank, Union Bank — all nationalized banks and NBFCs accept our DPR. PMEGP, Mudra, CGTMSE, Standup India are valid for all.
    Q
    In how many days will the project report be available?
    Your business details are delivered via WhatsApp in PDF format within 24–48 hours of receipt. Evening delivery is also possible in case of emergencies.
    Q
    When is the payment to be made?
    First, our team provides initial expert guidance to explain the entire process. Payment is made securely only when you are 100% satisfied with the prepared draft or print preview and want to proceed. There is no upfront charge for the primary review.
    Q
    Do I need to collect the documents physically or will they be available digitally?
    Both options are available – PDF will be delivered on WhatsApp/Email, and if desired, a physical copy will be delivered to your home via speed post.
